Panama

Panama is a country located in Central America, bordered by Costa Rica to the west and Colombia to the southeast. It is known for the Panama Canal, a significant artificial waterway that connects the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, facilitating international maritime trade.

Facts:

  • Capital: Panama City
  • Language: Spanish
  • Population: Approximately 4.5 million

Sites To See

Panama Canal, Panama Viejo, Biomueso

Things to Eat


Empanadas, Arros Con Pollo, Sancocho
